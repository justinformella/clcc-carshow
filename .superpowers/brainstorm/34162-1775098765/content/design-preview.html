★ ABOUT THE EVENT ★

Join us for the 4th annual Crystal Lake Cars & Caffeine charity car show in downtown Crystal Lake. Over 200 vehicles on display across 3 city blocks. Live awards, food vendors, and all net proceeds benefit the Crystal Lake Food Pantry.

$30 per vehicle · 100% to charity · Rain or shine

★ FAQ ★

► WHAT TIME SHOULD I ARRIVE?
Gates open at 7:30 AM. Show cars should arrive by 9:00 AM for placement.
► HOW MUCH DOES IT COST?
$30 per vehicle registration. Spectators are free! All proceeds go to the Crystal Lake Food Pantry.
► WHAT IF IT RAINS?
The show goes on rain or shine. No refunds for weather cancellations.
