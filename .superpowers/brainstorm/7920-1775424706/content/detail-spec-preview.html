Detail Tech — Design Summary

Review this before I write the full spec

Overview

New "Detail Tech" mini-game accessed from the action menu. Player details their car inside and out using a scratch-off mechanic — click to spray, drag to wipe away a grime overlay. Uses existing side view and dashboard pixel art. Ends with a showroom score and before/after reveal.

Canvas Effects

  • Grime overlay — semi-transparent brown/gray layer drawn over the car image. Erased by click (circle) or drag (path) using destination-out compositing.
  • Sparkle particles — white/gold bursts at wipe contact point. Same particle system as dyno exhaust.
  • Shine sweep — after cleaning, a diagonal white gradient animates across the car (wax finish effect).
  • Progress bar — percentage of grime removed, shown below the canvas.
  • Before/after split — sliding divider showing dirty vs clean side by side.
  • Spray sound — short noise burst on click. Squeegee/buff sound on drag.

Assets Needed

  • Detail bay background — one-time Imagen generation, stored in Supabase (like dyno room)
  • All car assets already exist — side view + dashboard view per car
  • No new car-specific art needed

Scoring

Score = percentage of grime removed across both passes (exterior 60% weight, interior 40% weight). Displayed as X/100 with a label: 95+ = "SHOWROOM PERFECT", 80-94 = "SHOW READY", 60-79 = "STREET CLEAN", <60 = "NEEDS WORK".
