Detail Tech — Game Flow

Inside & out detailing mini-game. Which flow feels right?

A

Exterior → Interior (Recommended)

Step 1: Wash — Car pulls in dirty. Spray/wipe the side view to remove grime layer.
Step 2: Polish — Buff the exterior for shine. Sparkle particles burst on contact.
Step 3: Interior — Switch to dashboard view. Wipe down the interior.
Step 4: Reveal — Shine sweep animation, showroom score, before/after split.

Pros

  • Natural detailing order
  • Uses both views
  • 3 distinct phases keep it fresh

Cons

  • Longer session (~45-60s)
B

Quick Detail (Single Phase)

One phase: Car pulls in. You wipe down the exterior only. Sparkles + score.
Interior is skipped to keep it short and snappy.

Pros

  • Fast (~20s)
  • Simple to build

Cons

  • Doesn't use dashboard view
  • Less satisfying
C

Full Service (4 Phases)

Step 1: Wash — Spray water to remove dirt
Step 2: Clay/Polish — Buff the exterior
Step 3: Wax — Apply wax coat with shine sweep
Step 4: Interior — Switch to dash, wipe down
Step 5: Reveal — Full before/after + score

Pros

  • Most realistic
  • Most content

Cons

  • Could feel repetitive
  • Longer build
