Weather & Rain Policy

üåßÔ∏è

Rain or Shine Event

Light Rain: The show goes on! We're car enthusiasts - a little water won't stop us.

Heavy Rain/Storms: If severe weather is forecasted, the event will be postponed to the following Sunday, May 24th, 2026.

Decision Timeline: Weather decisions will be made by 6:00 PM on Friday, May 15th and announced via:

  • Our website and social media channels
  • Email to all registered participants
  • Local radio stations (WZSR 105.5 FM)

Refund Policy: If the event is cancelled entirely (not postponed), full refunds will be issued within 10 business days.

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of vehicles can participate? ‚ñº
All vehicles are welcome! Classic cars, modern sports cars, muscle cars, imports, trucks, motorcycles, and anything with wheels that you're proud of. We celebrate all automotive enthusiasm!
Can I leave early if needed? ‚ñº
For safety reasons, we ask that all show vehicles remain in place until 2:00 PM. Emergency exits are available if absolutely necessary. Please inform staff if you need to leave early.
Are pets allowed at the event? ‚ñº
Leashed pets are welcome! Please keep them under control and clean up after them. Water stations for pets will be available throughout the venue.
Is there handicap accessibility? ‚ñº
Yes! We have designated handicap parking areas close to the show field, and all areas of the event are wheelchair accessible. Golf cart shuttles are also available for those who need assistance.
Can I sell parts or merchandise? ‚ñº
Yes, we have a designated swap meet area. Vendor spots are $50 and include a 10x10 space. Please register as a vendor in advance. No selling from show car spots please.
