About the Event

A Celebration of Automotive Excellence

Crystal Lake Cars & Caffeine has grown from a small gathering of local enthusiasts into one of the premier automotive events in McHenry County. Set against the charming backdrop of historic downtown Crystal Lake, our annual show brings together over 200 exceptional vehicles and thousands of spectators.

From pristine pre-war classics to cutting-edge supercars, from American muscle to Japanese imports, every vehicle tells a story of passion, dedication, and craftsmanship.

Be Prepared

Weather Policy

Rain or shine, we celebrate automotive passion

Light Rain

The show goes on! We're car enthusiasts—a little water won't stop us. Bring appropriate gear and enjoy the unique atmosphere.

Severe Weather

If severe weather is forecasted, the event will be postponed to Sunday, May 24th, 2026. Decision made by Friday 6:00 PM.

Updates

Weather decisions announced via our website, email to registered participants, and local radio (WZSR 105.5 FM).

Refunds

If the event is cancelled entirely (not postponed), full refunds will be issued within 10 business days.

Questions

Frequently Asked

What types of vehicles can participate? +
All vehicles are welcome! Classic cars, modern sports cars, muscle cars, imports, trucks, motorcycles, and anything with wheels that you're proud of. We celebrate all automotive enthusiasm. There are no year, make, or model restrictions.
Can I leave early if needed? +
For safety reasons, we ask that all show vehicles remain in place until 2:00 PM. This ensures the safety of spectators and maintains the integrity of the show. Emergency exits are available if absolutely necessary. Please inform staff at registration if you anticipate needing to leave early.
Are pets allowed at the event? +
Leashed pets are welcome! Please keep them under control at all times and clean up after them. Water stations for pets will be available throughout the venue. We ask that pets be kept away from show vehicles unless you're the owner.
Is there handicap accessibility? +
Yes, the event is fully accessible. We have designated handicap parking areas close to the show field, and all areas of the event are wheelchair accessible. Golf cart shuttles are available for those who need assistance getting around the venue.
Can I sell parts or merchandise? +
Yes, we have a designated swap meet area. Vendor spots are $50 and include a 10x10 space. Please register as a vendor in advance through our website. Selling from show car spots is not permitted to maintain the quality of the show field.
Is pre-registration required? +
Pre-registration is strongly encouraged to guarantee your spot. We have limited space for show vehicles, and historically we reach capacity before the event date. Day-of registration may be available but is not guaranteed.
